The problem

Every day you're losing money
you don't even know about

Service businesses lose thousands every month not because of bad work — but because of the gaps in between. Missed calls, cold quotes, late invoices. It adds up fast.

Missed calls = missed jobs

You're on a job, a call comes in, you can't answer. By the time you call back, they've already booked someone else. This happens every single day.

£300–£2,000 per missed call

Quotes go cold and die

You send a quote, hear nothing back, and move on. Most of those jobs were winnable — they just needed one follow-up that never came.

40–60% of quotes never followed up

Invoices sit unpaid

Chasing money is awkward. So you leave it. And it sits there. Meanwhile your cash flow suffers and you end up doing the awkward call anyway — weeks later.

Average 28 days to get paid

No reviews despite great work

Happy customers rarely leave reviews unless they're asked at exactly the right moment. That moment never comes, so your Google rating doesn't grow.

80% of reviews need asking for

Evenings lost to admin

Invoicing, booking, chasing, replying — it all happens after hours. You finish a long day on the tools and then spend two hours at a desk doing things that shouldn't need a human.

5–10 hours of admin every week

Old customers forgotten

Past clients who loved your work are sitting there. Nobody has reached out. A simple message at the right time would win back jobs — but it never gets sent.

5x cheaper to retain than acquire
